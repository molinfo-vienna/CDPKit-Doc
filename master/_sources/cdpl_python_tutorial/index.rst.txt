CDPL Python Tutorial
===========================

Welcome to the "Getting Started with CDPKit using Python" tutorial! If you're familiar with cheminformatics libraries like RDKit, you'll find that CDPKit offers a unique set of tools and functionalities that can take your computational drug design projects to new heights.

CDPKit, or Computational Drug Property Toolkit, is a powerful open-source library crafted for advanced drug discovery applications. Whether you're a researcher, a student, or an industry professional, this tutorial aims to introduce the basic concepts and functionalities of CDPKit, enabling you to harness its full potential.

In the subsequent sections, we'll walk you through the foundational concepts and dive into practical applications with hands-on examples.
By the end, you'll be well-equipped to incorporate CDPKit into your workflow and start crafting effective drug design solutions using Python.

So, grab a coffee (or tea) and let's embark on this exciting journey into the realm of computational drug design with CDPKit!
